Accountant Role Description
The accountant is responsible for preparing monthly, quarterly, and annual financial statements in accordance with International Financial Reporting Standards (IFRS).
The role includes maintaining accurate accounting records, managing the general ledger, and performing account reconciliations.
The accountant monitors and analyzes cash flow, operational expenses, and overall financial performance.
The role ensures compliance with banking regulations, internal policies, and statutory financial requirements.
The accountant prepares and submits VAT and corporate tax reports within required regulatory deadlines.
The role supports internal and external audits by providing accurate and complete financial documentation.
The accountant identifies financial discrepancies, risks, and internal control weaknesses and recommends improvements.

Reporting Structure
The accountant reports directly to the Finance Manager.
The role involves close collaboration with senior finance staff, auditors, and regulatory authorities to ensure financial accuracy, transparency, and compliance across banking operations.
